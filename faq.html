what is Kicharity

We created the Ch Charity World MasterCard that empowers our cardholders to earn 1% cash back in the form of a donation every time they make a purchase. Through our partnerships with GuideStar and Network for Good cardholder’s can choose any 501 (c) (3) organization, K-12 school, or religious organization to automatically donate their cash back to. We also enable cardholders to select up to three nonprofits at a time, so that every cause they care about can benefit each time they swipe their Charity Charge World MasterCard. Cardholders can track their impact on their donation dashboard.
