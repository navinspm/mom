Frequently Asked Questions

What is MOM?

MOM stands for MorefOrMankind – MOM was founded by a group of individuals, located in different parts of the world with a common objective to do something good for their fellow Mankind via a shared platform.

Why MOM?

In this day and age where everyone is doing a little something to support in a way they could, we often find that this kind of support is difficult to sustain long term. Moreover, a little appreciation from like-minded friends would help us stay motivated to continue working on a good cause. MOM was founded to bring together like minded friends with such philanthropic interests

How does MOM select to fund/support an initiative?

One of the founding members usually puts forth to the team, a concept that they would like to devote their attention to. While the team is always happy to pitch in and support a good cause, the actual help comes from those members with whom the concept resonates the most.

Is the support rendered by MOM tied to individuals or organizations?

MOM is happy to lend a helping hand whenever and wherever and whichever way possible. Our activity is restricted to neither an organization nor a geographical location

How do I become a member of MOM?

You don’t necessarily have to become a member if your aim is to help and support us on specific projects. If you do want to become a member, please contact us with your objective and samples of work done so far. Our founding members will decide on the outcome and inform you

Can I donate to MOM?

MOM is a charity initiative and not a registered trust at this stage and as such does not accept donations in the form of money. You are more than welcome to donate to a particular project that we are engaged in, in the form of your time and physical support

How do I obtain information on actively running projects?

Please refer to the active projects section on our web page for projects that are currently active. Our projects are diverse in nature and most often short term in nature.

Can I become a volunteer at MOM?

From time to time MOM has active projects that could benefit from volunteer help. If you are willing to donate your time and effort, please do look under the active projects section for the type of projects where your strengths could be leveraged and contact us via mail

How do I contact MOM?

You are more than welcome to email us at more4mankind@gmail.com with relevant details including a number we could reach you at
