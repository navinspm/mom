RAISING AWARENESS ON PERSONAL HYGIENE IN PRIMARY SCHOOL KIDS AND ITS IMPACT ON EDUCATION

With the help of MOM’s members, and members and friends from THOZHAMAI (ALUMNI of ARR School, Kumbakonam, batch of ‘87), Shruthi Yasothan from The Perse School, Cambridge, UK executed a project titled “BETTER HYGIENE FOR BETTER HEALTH” at The Kamarajar Mela Cauvery School in Kumbakonam in December 2016. Shruthi Yasothan is the daughter of Uma, one of our MOM’s founding members.

The workshop was intended to create awareness on the significance of maintaining personal hygiene with the help of audio visual aids, involving and integrating students, parents and teachers. Gift aids were distributed to about 60 students who attended the workshop which included equipments and household items they needed to practice what had been just preached.

It is never too early to involve growing children in charitable activities and in a way nurture them to see the world the way it needs to be seen — with kindness, compassion and care and also to step in where needed and we, here at MOM would like to support and encourage such endeavours
