Visit to Sai Ram old age home 13th of OCT 2018-10-14

“அன்போடு அறம் செய்”

It’s one thing to be left alone by immediate family to fend for yourselves when you grow old and it’s an entirely different thing when you don’t have a family to turn to in times of need. Priya from Sai ram old age homes offers a ray of hope to such old and needy to find a ‘home” so to speak at her own old age facility. Sai ram old age home was started nearly 4 years ago in memory of her grandmother and houses nearly 30 inmates now, a majority of whom stay there free of cost. Priya describes her day to day struggle of trying to provide them with a healthy meal three times a day with a special request for help for support during festival times.

Dr. Sumathi and Rajesh from MOM foundation visited the home on the 13th of October 2018 and conducted a medical camp, basically looking into their current health status, explaining their medical conditions if any, stressing on the importance of taking regular medications, but most of all encouraging and reassuring them. A kind word, a firm hand on the shoulder and some guidance with a lot of love is all that is needed to keep their morale going and we at MOM were happy that we were able to provide that on that day. Mr Saravanan, a friend of Rajesh who had heard about MOM donated cash towards their grocery expenses. We are glad that MOM continues to inspire members of the community to continue with their kind deeds. The event ended with Dr Sumathi celebrating her birthday at the venue. Thank you members of Sai ram home for your kind gesture. Kindness begets more kindness.
