More for mankind is a collective effort of a handful of friends from diverse backgrounds who would like to help their fellow mankind and view Philanthropy as their life’s fulfilment. This platform is a humble initial effort to formalise the various charitable activities we have been engaging in for a while now. At this stage, we certainly don’t know where this path is going to lead us but what we do know is we will get there whilst helping the needy with love and care.
Our areas of interests include 
  • Supporting education of girl children
  • Inculcating personal hygiene amongst girl children in semi urban areas
  • Enabling children with mental developmental challenges with a vocational life skill 
  • Appreciation and contribution to a fellow philanthropist
